How to cut an avocado the right way

On this page, you will learn how to correctly cut an avocado without getting injured. Have fun and stay safe!

Preparation

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 knife
  • 1 cutting board

Instructions

  1. Place the avocado on the cutting board
  2. Hold the avocado steady with one hand
  3. With the other hand, carefully cut around the pit of the avocado
  4. Twist the two halves to separate them
  5. Remove the pit with a spoon or knife

Have a delicious avocado without any hospital visits!
